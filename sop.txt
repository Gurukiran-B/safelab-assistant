FIRE IN LAB SOP (fire, lab fire, fire accident):

Step 1: Raise alarm and inform everyone in the lab
Step 2: Switch off gas and electrical supply if safe
Step 3: Use fire extinguisher if fire is small
Step 4: Evacuate immediately through emergency exit
Step 5: Inform lab in-charge

Warning: Do not use water on electrical fire

---

CHEMICAL SPILL SOP (chemical spill, acid spill, chemical leak):

Step 1: Wear gloves and safety equipment
Step 2: Identify the chemical
Step 3: Use spill kit to contain the spill
Step 4: Avoid direct contact
Step 5: Inform lab supervisor

Warning: Do not inhale fumes
